About

Attendance management system is designed to keep track of attendance of students enrolled in different courses. This system facilitate management of students, teachers and courses data.

In attendance management system, scope is that one teacher will take only one course. It has two users, admin and teacher. Admin has below functionalites :

  1. add teacher
  2. edit teacher
  3. delete teacher
  4. add course
  5. edit course
  6. delete course
  7. add student
  8. edit student
  9. delete student

Teacher can choose a date and mark attendance of students for that date. And the teacher can check overall attendance percentage of all the students enrolled in that course
